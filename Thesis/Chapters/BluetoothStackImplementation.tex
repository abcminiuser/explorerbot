\lhead{Chapter \ref{chp:btstackimp}. \emph{Bluetooth Stack Implementation}}

The development of the Bluetooth stack followed a bottom-up development methodology, designing and writing each software layer in sequence from the lowest layer first, to the highest and most abstracted layers last. This sequence ensured that each layer was functionally correct and could be verified before the higher layers were implemented. This suited the development of the Bluetooth stack---and, indeed, most software stacks---as each software layer is dependant solely on lower logical layers in the stack.
